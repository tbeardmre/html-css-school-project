About

the creation of Shadow Reels

Shadow Reels is a content creation company located in Victoria, British Columbia. The inspiration for the company came to our owner, Tom Beardmore, through his combined passions for mountain biking, photography & videography, and the great outdoors. While this rests at the heart of Shadow Reels' inception, we have expanded to serve a diverse scope of customers across Vancouver Island and the Lower Mainland. At Shadow Reels, we strive to create captivating content for a wide range of companies, events, and personal pursuits. Most importantly, we understand what passion means, and we are here to help you convey yours.

Work

2021

VBIS

Project: Toss The Boss

Toss The Boss is a fundraising event that challenges teams to raise funds in support of the Victoria Brain Injury Society & the Nanaimo Brain Injury Society. Shadow Reels worked closely with VBIS and WildPlay to create multiple promotional videos to spread awarness about brain injury. The project was completed in approximately 9 months, it included a short video showcasing the fundraising event as well as a video series shedding light on brain injury survivor stories.

2022

akta

Project: Launch Video

This year the company "akta" is releasing their first product launch of mountain bike gear. The company has sponsored multiple riders from different parts of BC, it was the job of Shadow Reels to provide filmmaking services for all the riders that live on Vancouver Island. The full video is still in production and will be released near the end of 2022.
